## What is numerology? Definition and origins

Numerology is a mathematical system for interpreting numbers that analyzes personality and destiny from a date of birth and a full name. Unlike astrology, which is based on planetary positions, numerology uses only reproducible arithmetic calculations. Karmastro computes these numbers with [verifiable precision](/en/precision).

This millennia-old discipline has its roots in the work of **Pythagoras** (around 580-495 BC), the famous Greek mathematician who claimed that "everything is number." The Babylonians already used a numerical system based on the vibration of numbers more than 4000 years ago.

Modern numerology distinguishes two great schools:
- **The Pythagorean method**: uses numbers 1 to 9 plus the master numbers 11, 22, 33
- **The Chaldean method**: older, assigns different values to letters

According to studies of contemporary spiritual interest, a growing share of Western populations is interested in numerology, particularly the 25-45 age group who see it as a more rational approach than traditional astrology.

## How numerology works: the mathematical basics

Numerology rests on the principle of **theosophical reduction**: you add up all the digits of a number until you get a single digit from 1 to 9, except for the master numbers 11, 22, and 33, which keep their value.

**Calculation example**:
- Date: 15/07/1985
- Addition: 1+5+0+7+1+9+8+5 = 36
- Reduction: 3+6 = 9
- Life path: 9

This mathematical method guarantees that every calculation can be verified and reproduced, unlike subjective interpretations. That is what makes numerology accessible even to the most skeptical.

## The fundamental numbers in numerology

Numerology analyzes **five main numbers** extracted from your personal data:

### The Life Path

Calculated from your complete date of birth, it reveals your main mission in this existence. It is the most important number in numerology.

### The Expression Number

Obtained by adding all the letters of your full birth name, it indicates your natural talents and your expression capacity.

### The Soul Number

Calculated from the vowels of your name, it reveals your deep desires and inner motivations.

### The Personality Number

Based on the consonants of your name, it shows how others perceive you and your social image.

### The Personal Year Number

It changes every year and indicates the energies and opportunities of the current year.

Each number brings a different light on your personality, creating a **complete numerological profile** of five dimensions.

## How to calculate your life path: a simple method

The **life path** is the most fundamental calculation in numerology. Here is the step-by-step method:

**Step 1**: Write your full date of birth
- Example: November 28, 1992

**Step 2**: Convert to digits only
- 28/11/1992

**Step 3**: Add all the digits
- 2+8+1+1+1+9+9+2 = 33

**Step 4**: Reduce if necessary
- 33 is a master number: keep 33
- If it were 34: 3+4 = 7

### Alternative method (more precise)

Some numerologists prefer to add separately day, month, and year:
- Day: 28 = 2+8 = 10 = 1+0 = 1
- Month: 11 (master number, kept)
- Year: 1992 = 1+9+9+2 = 21 = 2+1 = 3
- Total: 1+11+3 = 15 = 1+5 = 6

This method allows preserving intermediate master numbers for a finer analysis.

## FAQ

### Is numerology an exact science?
Numerology uses reproducible mathematical calculations, but interpretation remains subjective. It is a personal development tool based on symbolic correspondences, not a science in the strict sense.

### Can you change your life path?
No, the life path is fixed because calculated on your birth date. However, you can evolve in how you express it and develop its positive aspects throughout your life.

### What is the difference between Pythagorean and Chaldean numerology?
The Pythagorean method (the most widespread) uses numbers 1-9 + master numbers, while the Chaldean method assigns different values to letters and excludes 9. Both are valid according to the chosen tradition.

### Can numerology predict the future?
Numerology reveals trends and cycles, not specific events. It helps you understand your potentials and the energies of a given period, but does not predict the future deterministically.

### Do you need to be a believer to practice numerology?
Absolutely not. Numerology can be approached as a psychological tool of self-knowledge, based on objective calculations. Even the most rational people often find interesting elements of reflection in it.
